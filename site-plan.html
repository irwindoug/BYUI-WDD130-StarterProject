Overview

Purpose

Our purpose here at Dry Oar Rafting is to provide our guests with unforgettable memories! Through our once-in-a-lifetime experiences, our hope is to encourage people to be able to experience the outdoors in the way it is meant to be experienced. With one of our expert guides as they give a history of the famous landscape, you can never expect to have the same experience twice! We hope you can come enjoy a once in a lifetime trip that will give you the air in your face and the excitement of a lifetime and maybe a mouth full of water as you have a cold splash.

Audience

Are you a thrill seeker, an adventurer or maybe the silent observer? No matter if you are looking for fun or maybe something new, this is for you. Enjoy a little of everything! The nice relaxing view from the rafting with amazing views and sites with history with it. You will love the adventure through the spoken words of your guide to paint a picture in your head of what once was. Enjoy the silence and peace of nature given by the sounds of the earth to a roaring water of current that will keep you on the hedge of your seat. If you’re ready for an experience of your life we invite one and all to enjoy an experience and time of your life you won’t forget.

The Site Map of a site is just like it sounds…it is a map of the pages in a site and how they are related and linked together. From the map above we can see that we will eventually have the Home page and 2 sub or child pages.

The lines that connect them all together indicate that each page should be accessible from any other page, it is essentially showing us the global navigation for the site.

Wireframes

Wireframes are like blueprints for making webpages. They should show the major sections of content that will be on the page and the relative locations of each element. In the wireframe below you can see there will be 6 sections to our page:

  1. At the top we have a section with the logo (the box with the mountain means an image) and the navigation bar.
  2. Then there is a banner image that stretches all the way across the screen.
  3. Next we have some text and an image
  4. ...followed by another row made up of an image and some text.
  5. Then one more section of text with no image.
  6. Lastly, a footer containing a copyright/name line and 3 social media icons.
